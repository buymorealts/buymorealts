Step 4: Purchase Altcoins

You can now transfer your Bitcoin from Coinbase to Binance and begin buying altcoins!

1

Open your Binance wallet

To do this, click Funds in the menu bar and select Deposits Withdrawals.

This will display all of the available coins you can purchase on Binance, and show how much of each coin you currently hold.

Binance generates a Bitcoin wallet address that can be used to send Bitcoin so that the Bitcoin is on your Binance account. To learn more about wallets, click here.

To view your unique Bitcoin wallet address on Binance, scroll down to Bitcoin and click Deposit.



This will show your Binance Bitcoin wallet address.



We will now send our Bitcoin from Coinbase to this address.

2

On Coinbase, click

This will open your wallet on Coinbase and will show you the amount of each coin you have. In your Bitcoin wallet, click Send.




3

Send Bitcoin to Binance

Remember the Bitcoin wallet address we generated on Binance in Step 1? Copy that address and paste it in the Recipient box.


Next, enter the amount of Bitcoin you would like to send to Binance and click Continue.

4

Wait for your coins to arrive to Binance

Depending on the network traffic, this could take anywhere from a few seconds to a few minutes. You can track your transaction by pasting your Binance wallet address on www.blockchain.info. The balance on your Binance wallet will update once the transaction goes through.

5

Buy your altcoins!

On the Binance menu bar, click Exchange and choose Basic. On the right side of the trading dashboard, you can select your desired altcoin from the list:




Once you select an altcoin, you can complete a Buy Order. Your total available Bitcoin balance will be displayed to you (it will be the amount you sent from Coinbase earlier).

Enter the desired amount you'd like to buy of the altcoin, and click Buy!
